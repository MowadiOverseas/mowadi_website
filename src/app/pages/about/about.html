About Mowadi Overseas

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Mowadi Overseas Ltd. has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted recruitment agencies, specializing in deploying skilled domestic helpers, cleaning staff, and professional workers to the Middle East.

Based in Nairobi, we have built strong partnerships with employers across Saudi Arabia, UAE, and other Gulf countries, successfully placing over 5,000 workers in rewarding positions abroad.

Our commitment to integrity, professionalism, and reliability has earned us the trust of both employers seeking quality staff and workers seeking legitimate opportunities for better lives.
